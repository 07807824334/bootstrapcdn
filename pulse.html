What is PULSE?

In the spirt of community openness, PULSE is a set of real-time dashboards showing overall BootstrapCDN activity. The fine folks at Leftronic are providing visualizations for BootstrapCDN bandwidth, popular files, daily/weekly/monthly reports, and pageviews. Jump into any of the dashboards below.
